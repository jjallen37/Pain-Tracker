How to Support the Paintracking Project

The Paintracking Project is committed to helping people with chronic pain improve their life quality by making sense of their experience.

The author, Deborah Barrett, has donated the entire proceeds of the book to this site, and in particular to the development and maintenance of the “paintracker” application. When the costs exceed book proceeds (as is currently the case), the remaining funds come from the author's own pocket. If you would like to help defray costs, and allow for continued improvements to the site’s capabilities, consider supporting the site.

Online donations can be made through PayPal. If you don’t have a PayPal account, you can use a credit card on the PayPal site.
